Details about our Library

We are a community library dedicated to spreading knowledge and inspiring a love for reading.

Our shelves hold books for every age, along with digital resources to support learning.

We host events, workshops, and activities that bring people together and spark curiosity.


Our mission

"Welcoming space where ideas grow and stories live on".


Main Goals

  1. Help people enjoy reading.

  2. Give books and resources to everyone.

  3. Organize events for the community.

  4. Use new technology but keep the love of books alive.

Library Timings

Monday to Friday: 9 AM - 6 PM

Saturday: 9 AM - 1 PM

Sunday: Closed
